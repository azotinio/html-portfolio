Spaghetti carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, and bacon is replaced by chicken.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • 50g of your fav cheese
  • 1 chicken breast
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted boiling water.
  2. Cook breast in water and cut into small pieces.
  3. Mix eggs and grated cheese in a bowl.
  4. Combine pasta with chicken and egg mixture.
  5. Season with salt and pepper and serve.
  6. I add tomato sauce so it is not carbonara but my original recipe

Omelette

A hearty breakfast, day starter.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggs
  • half cup of milk
  • sausage, tomatoes
  • salt and pepper
  • avocado oil or sunflower oil

Steps

  1. Pour some oil on pan.
  2. Mix eggs and milk in bowl.
  3. Cut the tomato and sausages in pieces and into bowl.
  4. Pour that mix on pan and sear on both sides.
  5. Add salt and pepper.

Pancakes

Soft and fluffy pancakes, perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • Butter for frying

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, and baking powder in a bowl.
  2. Add egg and milk, then whisk until smooth.
  3. Heat a pan and add butter.
  4. Pour batter into the pan.
  5. Cook until golden on both sides.
